About the Khanqah

A Place of Light & Guidance

Khanqah Maseehul Ummat is a blessed center of Islah, Tarbiyat, and spiritual development. Under the guidance of our respected Shuyukh, this Khanqah serves as a place where hearts find peace, souls are purified, and the connection with Allah ﷻ is strengthened.

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْنَا مِنَ الصَّالِحِينَ

"O Allah, make us among the righteous."
